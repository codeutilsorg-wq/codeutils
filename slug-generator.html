About URL Slug Generator

This tool converts text into URL-friendly slugs, perfect for blog posts, product pages, and SEO-optimized URLs.

What is a Slug?

A slug is the part of a URL that identifies a page in a human-readable form. For example, in https://example.com/my-blog-post, "my-blog-post" is the slug.

Best Practices

  • Keep slugs short and descriptive
  • Use hyphens instead of underscores (Google preference)
  • Use lowercase letters only
  • Include relevant keywords for SEO
  • Avoid special characters and spaces
