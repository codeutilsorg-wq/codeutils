About Epoch Time

Epoch time (Unix timestamp) is the number of seconds since January 1, 1970 00:00:00 UTC.

Common Timestamps

Epoch Date
0Jan 1, 1970 00:00:00 UTC
1000000000Sep 9, 2001 01:46:40 UTC
1609459200Jan 1, 2021 00:00:00 UTC
2147483647Jan 19, 2038 03:14:07 UTC (Y2K38)

Formats

  • Seconds: 10 digits (e.g., 1609459200)
  • Milliseconds: 13 digits (e.g., 1609459200000)
