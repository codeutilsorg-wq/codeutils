About Word Counter

This tool counts various text statistics in real-time as you type or paste text.

Statistics Explained

  • Words: Number of words separated by spaces
  • Characters: Total character count including spaces
  • Characters (no spaces): Character count excluding spaces
  • Sentences: Counted by sentence-ending punctuation (. ! ?)
  • Paragraphs: Blocks of text separated by blank lines
  • Lines: Number of line breaks + 1
  • Reading Time: Based on average reading speed of 200 words/min
  • Speaking Time: Based on average speaking speed of 150 words/min
