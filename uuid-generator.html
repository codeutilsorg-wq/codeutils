About UUID Generator

This tool generates random UUID v4 (Universally Unique Identifiers). UUIDs are 128-bit numbers used to uniquely identify information in computer systems.

Features

  • UUID v4: Generates cryptographically random UUIDs
  • Bulk Generation: Generate up to 1000 UUIDs at once
  • Format Options: Uppercase, with or without dashes
  • Copy & Download: Easily copy or download generated UUIDs

UUID v4 Format

UUID v4 follows the format: xxxxxxxx-xxxx-4xxx-yxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx

  • The "4" indicates version 4 (random)
  • The "y" is one of 8, 9, A, or B (variant indicator)
  • All other characters are random hexadecimal digits

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+Enter: Generate single UUID
  • Ctrl+K: Copy UUID to clipboard
