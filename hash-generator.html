About Hash Generator

Generate cryptographic hashes from text using MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, and SHA-512 algorithms. All processing happens in your browser.

Hash Algorithms

  • MD5 (128-bit): Fast but not cryptographically secure. Good for checksums.
  • SHA-1 (160-bit): Faster than SHA-2 but considered weak for security.
  • SHA-256 (256-bit): Widely used and secure. Recommended for most applications.
  • SHA-512 (512-bit): More secure with larger output. Good for high-security applications.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+Enter: Generate hashes
