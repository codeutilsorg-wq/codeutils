About Meta Tags

Meta tags provide information about your webpage to search engines and social media platforms.

Important Meta Tags

  • Title: Displayed in search results and browser tabs
  • Description: Summary shown in search results
  • Open Graph: Controls how links appear on Facebook/LinkedIn
  • Twitter Card: Controls how links appear on Twitter
