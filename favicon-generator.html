About Favicons

Favicons are small icons displayed in browser tabs, bookmarks, and address bars.

Recommended Sizes

  • 16x16: Browser tabs (traditional)
  • 32x32: Taskbar shortcuts, high-DPI tabs
  • 64x64: High-resolution displays
  • 180x180: Apple Touch Icon
  • 512x512: PWA icons
